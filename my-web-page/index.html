Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about my services and workflow

My primary tools are Blender for modeling and sculpting, ZBrush for high-detail work, Substance Painter for texturing, and Maya for complex rigging. For VTuber avatars, I often use Unity or Unreal Engine for final implementation.

A full 3D VTuber avatar typically takes 4-6 weeks from concept to completion, depending on complexity. 2D Live2D models usually take 2-3 weeks. Rush options may be available for an additional fee.
